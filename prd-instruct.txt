PRD: Mestej Winery Website
1. Vision & Goals

The website aims to present Mestej’s local honey, buckthorn, and blueberry wines in a modern yet classic digital experience. The design emphasizes clarity, simplicity, and elegance while integrating a parallax effect and translucent UI elements.

Goals:

Showcase Mestej’s story, history, and premium wine products.

Provide intuitive age-gated access ensuring compliance with alcohol laws.

Offer a multilingual experience (English & Swedish).

Create strong brand identity with black + golden theme, white text.

Drive engagement through promo video, social media links, and Systembolaget integration.

Prepare for future online ordering functionality and webshop for merchandise.

2. Design Principles

Minimalist Layout: uncluttered, parallax scrolling.

Typography: modern serif for headings, sans-serif for body text.

Color Palette:

Background: Black (#000000)

Accent: Gold (#FFD700 or refined gold tone)

Text: White (#FFFFFF)

Navigation: centered menu items, concise labels.

Consistency: responsive across desktop, tablet, and mobile.

3. Features & Requirements
3.1 Age Verification

Requirement: Users must confirm legal drinking age before accessing content.

Logic:

If confirmed → enter main site.

If denied → show custom message, restrict access.

3.2 Main Page

Content:

Brief introduction to Mestej.

Product showcase (meads: honey, buckthorn, blueberry wines).

Restaurant availability info.

Systembolaget link.

Prominent promo video (autoplay muted).

TikTok & Instagram links.

Style: Hero video/image background, translucent overlay.

3.3 Navigation Menu

Items (centered, simple):

About Mestej

History

Wines & Liquors

Contact

Login (permit holders)

Online Ordering (placeholder)

Webshop

3.4 Sub-Pages

About Mestej: short company story.

History: heritage, origin of meads.

Wines & Liquors: product gallery + Systembolaget links.

Contact: contact form, map, email.

Login: restricted to permit holders.

Online Ordering: placeholder for search/cart/purchase flow.

Webshop: merchandise products (shirts, glasses, corkscrews, etc.).

3.5 Localization

Languages: English (default) & Swedish.

Language toggle in top-right corner.

All content translatable.

3.6 Error Handling

Age verification failure: “Sorry, you must be of legal drinking age to enter.”

Product unavailable: “Currently unavailable. Please check back soon.”
